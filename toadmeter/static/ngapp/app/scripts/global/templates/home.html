Have you ever had a feeling "here is something that I want and can afford, but still it is too expensive, so shouldnt buy it"? In russian this feeling describes as "to be strangled by a toad". Toadmeter is a simple app that can help you to tame this evil toad by controlling your expenses.

Yes, there are plenty of such apps. I don't pretend this one to be the best or even good enough, it was developed mostly for experience and my own need to have configurable visualization.

Toadmeter can log your incomes and expences (or import them from CSV generated by other apps) and show them as list or as configurable graphs.

To use toadmeter you have to register, but it is simple, just username and password.
